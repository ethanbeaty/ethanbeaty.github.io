ABOUT ME

I love poetry

Ethan Beaty





Born and raised in Southwest Louisiana, I never thought I'd write poetry. My first poems were ones I was required to write in middle school, and I started writing in my free time during high school. Love, myth, religion, philosophy, and language have long been interests, and poetry was a way for me to tie them all together in sometimes unconventional ways. The name Ilya was given to me by my Russian group, and stuck with me since- enjoy!
